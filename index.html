ABOUT RESEARCH TEACHING MISC.
Hi there. Thanks for stopping by. :)

I'm Yimin Xiao, an incoming PhD student in Information Studies at UMD iSchool. I use mixed methods to research on computer-mediated communication (CMC), computer-supported cooperative work (CSCW), human-computer interaction (HCI) , and applied linguistics. I aim to understand how digital technologies mediate human communicatiion and collaboration, analyze how people adopt and engage with these technologies, and identify future design directions to support human interaction. When not doing research, I'm probably photographing (aka, taking/editing random photos on my phone), journaling, reading, visiting museums, exploring new places, and binge-watching my favorite shows. I am a proud SU Orange, SISUer, & UNCW Seahawk.
