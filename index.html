Hi, I'm Yimin Xiao, pronounced as [i:min hsiao]. I am a master's student at Syracuse University studying Applied Data Science and Linguistics. Previously, I studied at Shanghai International Studies University for my Bachelor's degrees, at Fudan University for a minor certificate, and at University of North Carolina Wilmington as a visting student.

I am interested in the social dynamic of computer-mediated communication, computer supported cooeperative work, and online communities. I study the people communicating in these contexts, the information they construct and spread, and the network and communities they form. I use mixed methods for my research - quantitative analysis, experiments, etc. Read more about my research and publication here.

I enjoy reading travel writings, fictions, and non-finctions. Words carry weight, beauty, and joy. I also like walking in cities and in the nature, to feel the world we live in.
