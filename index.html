YIMIN XIAO

Hello. I'm a second-year PhD student in Information Studies at University of Maryland, College of Information Studies (iSchool). I research on human-computer interaction for computer-mediated multilingual communication - to understand and provide technology design solutions for people to communicate across linguistic boundaries.

I hold MS in Applied Data Science, MA in Linguistic Studies, and CAS in Language Teaching from Syracuse University, BM(anagement) in Information Management and System and BA in English with a minor in Finance from Shanghai International Studies University (SISU). I was an international visiting student at University of North Carolina Wilmington (UNCW) in Fall 2016.

I have profound curiosity to explore inward about myself and outward about the world. I take walks in the nature, read, journal, visit museum, experience and create art, and nap. I document my exploration through writing and photograph.
